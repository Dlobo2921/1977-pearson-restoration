# Restoration Timeline

Starting point: weekend of June 21–22, 2026. Upholstery materials already in hand (foam + all fabrics).

---

## Weekend — June 21–22 (Saturday & Sunday)

**Goal: Cushions out, fabric apart, patterns ready to trace Monday**

- [ ] Remove ALL cushions — photograph each one in place before pulling it out
- [ ] Label every cushion with masking tape + marker as you remove it: "S1-Back", "S1-Bottom", "V-Berth Port", "V-Berth Stbd", "QBerth", etc.
- [ ] Use seam ripper to carefully open every seam — keep the panels intact, they ARE your patterns
- [ ] Lay each panel flat, smooth, and label it: "S1-Back-FACE", "S1-Back-BOXING", etc.
- [ ] Stack by cushion, rubber-band or bag together

---

## Monday June 23

### Morning — Pattern Tracing & Cutting

- [ ] Lay each panel on kraft paper or Tyvek and trace the shape
- [ ] Mark seam allowances on the pattern (confirm with upholsterer — typically 1/2")
- [ ] Label every pattern piece; add grain/nap direction arrows
- [ ] Cut out patterns and test-lay on new fabric before cutting

### Afternoon — Begin Salon Cleaning

Everything is out. Work top to bottom, port to starboard.

**Sequence:**

1. **Vacuum** — dry debris, corners, lockers, under all shelf lips
2. **Fiberglass** — Star Brite solution + Magic Eraser; scrub, wipe down with microfiber
3. **Teak joinery** — diluted Murphy Oil Soap, soft brush, scrub WITH the grain, wipe DRY immediately (do not let it sit wet)
4. **Headliner** — damp microfiber only, no saturating
5. **End of day** — apply 303 Aerospace Protectant to all fiberglass and vinyl once dry

---

## Tuesday June 24

### Head / Bathroom

- [ ] Remove old hoses — photograph routing and size before pulling (or mark with tape)
- [ ] Measure and cut replacement hoses (Trident #102 sanitation hose or equivalent)
- [ ] Install new hose clamps (316 SS, double-clamp all connections below waterline)
- [ ] Clean fiberglass and vinyl — same sequence as salon (vacuum → fiberglass → vinyl → 303)
- [ ] Assess head floor vinyl — replace or treat with 303
- [ ] Allow to dry with companionway open overnight

---
