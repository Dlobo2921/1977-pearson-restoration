# 10 Powerful Claude Commands

> Simple commands that make Claude more useful, more focused, and more strategic.

| # | Command | Tag | What it does | Best for |
|---|---------|-----|--------------|----------|
| 1 | `/ghost` | Write More Human | Makes output sound natural and conversational | Posts, emails, newsletters, thought leadership |
| 2 | `/roast` | Get Brutally Honest Feedback | Exposes weak spots, blind spots, and vague thinking | Copy, decks, landing pages, content |
| 3 | `/matrix` | Compare Options Clearly | Turns messy choices into side-by-side comparisons | Software, hiring, strategy, vendor decisions |
| 4 | `/steal` | Reverse-Engineer What Works | Pulls out the principles behind winning content or products | Campaigns, landing pages, viral ideas |
| 5 | `/brief` | Cut The Fluff | Delivers short, direct, actionable answers | Summaries, decisions, key takeaways |
| 6 | `/focus` | Keep The Model On Track | Reduces rambling and improves relevance | Strategy, planning, research, technical work |
| 7 | `/challenge` | Pressure-Test Your Thinking | Forces objections, risks, and failure points into view | Ideas, pricing, products, business decisions |
| 8 | `/expand` | Go Deeper | Adds explanation, examples, and implementation detail | When the first answer is right but too shallow |
| 9 | `/systemize` | Turn Tasks Into Workflows | Helps build repeatable processes instead of one-off outputs | Content, onboarding, lead qualification, meetings |
| 10 | `/ghost` + `/brief` + `/challenge` | Power Combo | Humanize the writing, shorten the answer, then stress-test the idea | Creators, operators, founders |

---

**The Real Advantage:** Better AI results usually come from better instructions, better structure, and better workflows. The model matters. But how you think with it matters more.
